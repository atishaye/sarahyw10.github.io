State and Local Elections State and local elections can take place in any year, at various times throughout the year. There can be statewide elections for governor or state legislature. A city may elect its mayor. There may be races for judges and local officials. Ballot initiatives may be up for a vote.
Congressional Elections Congressional elections occur every two years. Voters choose one-third of senators and every member of the House of Representatives.
Presidental Elections Presidental elections occur every four years. Voters vote in primaries where they select who they want to be the presidential canidate for their party. Then during the Presidental election voters select their canidate
